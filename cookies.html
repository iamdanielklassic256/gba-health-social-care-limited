1. Introduction

GBA Health Social Care Limited (“we”, “our”, “us”) values your privacy. We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience, provide personalized services, and analyze website performance. This Cookies Policy explains how we collect and use cookies, and how you can manage them.

2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small data files stored on your device when you visit a website. They enable websites to recognize your device, remember your preferences, and provide tailored experiences. Cookies help us improve functionality and deliver relevant content.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for core website functions such as navigation, security, and user authentication.
  • Performance Cookies: Gather anonymous data about website usage to help us improve site performance and user experience.
  • Functionality Cookies: Remember your preferences, such as language selection or location, to enhance your experience.
  • Advertising Cookies: Track your interactions with ads to provide relevant marketing content.

4. Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services, such as analytics providers and advertising partners, who place cookies on your device to help us analyze site performance and deliver targeted content. These third-party cookies are subject to the respective provider's privacy policies.

5. Managing Cookies

You have the option to accept, reject, or delete cookies via your browser settings. Please note that some website features may not function correctly if cookies are disabled. For more guidance, visit your browser's help section.

6. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookies Policy periodically. Changes will be reflected on this page along with the revised effective date. We encourage you to review this page regularly for updates.
