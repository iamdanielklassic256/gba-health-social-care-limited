At GBA Health Social Care Limited, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you interact with us.

1. Information We Collect

We collect various types of information to provide you with our comprehensive health and social care services:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number, and postal address
  • Professional Information: Details submitted through career application forms, including CVs and cover letters
  • Communication Data: Information from your inquiries, feedback, and correspondence with us
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information, and pages visited on our website
  • Usage Analytics: How you interact with our website and services to improve user experience

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information for legitimate business purposes, including:

  • Service Delivery: To provide our health and social care consulting services
  • Communication: To respond to your enquiries, provide customer support, and send important updates
  • Recruitment: To process job applications and evaluate candidates for employment opportunities
  • Service Improvement: To enhance our website functionality and service offerings
  • Legal Compliance: To fulfill our legal obligations and regulatory requirements in the healthcare sector
  • Marketing: To send relevant information about our services (only with your consent)

3. Sharing of Information

We respect your privacy and do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share your data only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party vendors who help us deliver our services (under strict confidentiality agreements)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government regulation
  • Business Protection: To protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of our clients and employees
  • Regulatory Bodies: With healthcare regulatory authorities as required for compliance purposes

All third parties we work with are required to maintain the confidentiality and security of your personal information.

4. Your Rights

Under data protection laws, you have several important rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right of Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to Rectification: Ask us to correct any inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data in certain circumstances
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Ask us to limit how we use your information
  • Right to Data Portability: Request your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Right to Object: Object to our processing of your personal data for specific purposes
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Remove your consent for marketing communications at any time

Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights or if you have questions about our privacy practices, please contact us:

gbahealthsocialcareltd@gmail.com

5. Security Measures

We take the security of your personal data seriously and implement robust measures to protect it:

  • Technical Safeguards: Encryption, secure servers, and firewalls to protect against unauthorized access
  • Access Controls: Strict limitations on who can access your personal information within our organization
  • Staff Training: Regular training for all employees on data protection and privacy practices
  • Regular Audits: Periodic security assessments and updates to our protection measures
  • Incident Response: Established procedures to respond quickly to any potential data breaches

While we implement industry-standard security measures, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We continuously work to improve our security practices to protect your information.

6. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, legal requirements, or business operations. When we make changes:

  • Notification: We will post the updated policy on this page with a new "Last updated" date
  • Significant Changes: For major changes, we may notify you directly via email or prominent website notices
  • Continued Use: Your continued use of our services after any changes indicates your acceptance of the updated policy
  • Review Regularly: We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information

Questions or Concerns?

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data handling practices, we're here to help.
